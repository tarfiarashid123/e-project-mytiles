  • 24" x 24" - All color variants

Installation Guidelines

For best results, follow these installation guidelines:

  1. Ensure substrate is clean, dry, and level (max. deviation 3mm over 2m)
  2. Use high-quality thin-set mortar suitable for ceramic tiles
  3. Maintain joint width of 3-5mm for proper grouting
  4. Allow adhesive to cure for 24 hours before grouting
  5. Use flexible grout for areas subject to movement

Maintenance

To maintain the beauty of your tiles:

  • Clean regularly with pH-neutral cleaner
  • Avoid abrasive cleaners or steel wool
  • Wipe up spills promptly, especially acidic substances
  • For polished finish, periodically apply tile sealer
