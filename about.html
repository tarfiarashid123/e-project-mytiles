About Our Tile Company

For over two decades, we've been transforming spaces with our premium quality tiles, combining timeless craftsmanship with innovative design.

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Premium Tiles began as a small family business with a passion for beautiful spaces. What started as a modest showroom in downtown has now grown into one of the nation's leading tile suppliers.

Our journey has been guided by a simple principle: quality matters. From sourcing the finest materials to perfecting our installation techniques, every step reflects our commitment to excellence.

Today, we serve homeowners, architects, and contractors across the country, offering an unparalleled selection of ceramic, porcelain, glass, and natural stone tiles for every style and budget.
